Jennie's Math 10 Commandments

I.

I am your math class, you shall afford to me equal or greater time
as compared to your other activities, classes, jobs or obligations.


II.

Thou shalt not procrastinate.


III.

Thou shalt not pretend to study for math while actually doing other things.


IV.

Remember to do math everyday, and keep this ritual holy.


V.

Honor thy notes and syllabus.


VI.

Thou shalt not scribble math and think that this is enough to have internalized it.


VII.

Thou shalt not cheat.


VIII.

Thou shalt not take a peer's solution as one's own.


IX.

Thou shalt be honest with oneself about how much math one has truly mastered.


X.

Thou shalt not compare thy's math performance with the performance of others.
