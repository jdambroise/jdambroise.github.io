In addition to homework problems you hand in for credit,
below is a list of additional selected problems to help you study.

In the textbook, you should also do the "Test Yourself" sections.
Textbook exercises with blue letters or numbers have solutions provided, and
the symbol H indicates only a hint or partial solution is given in the textbook.

Keep in mind you should not rely heavily on the textbook to always provide the answers for you.
Work on thinking and writing independently about the problems on your own,
and developing your own mathematically correct style.
Reach out for help to discuss when something doesn't make sense.

    Here is a self-reflection rating scale you can use.
  • 4 - I can show other students how to do this problem.
  • 3 - I can do this problem entirely on my own.
  • 2 - I need more practice.
  • 1 - I need help.
