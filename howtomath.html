Rough Stages of Learning Math

I. Learn Snippets (before class)

  • read the book in advance
  • write pencil notes in book margins (or use a notebook if you prefer)
    • ? question marks when you don't understand
    • ✔ check marks when you get it
    • circle important concepts or words that are not already emphasized in the text
    • summarize the section

II. Learn Details (in & after class)

  • attend all lectures and take notes
  • read book again
    • make connections between book margin notes (see above) and lecture notes
    • replace question marks in margins (see above) with answers & clarification
    • record new questions addressing deeper issues
  • discuss!
    • in office hours
    • with friends
    • with yourself

III. Integration & Practice (homeworks)

  • notice how much work should be done before homeworks!!
  • drill problems
  • how does the problem relate to your lecture notes and margin snippets?
    • what section is this problem from?
    • what concept is this problem implementing?
  • discuss!
    • in office hours
    • with friends
    • with yourself

IV. Big Picture (post-homework)

  • review, refine and revise your notes
    • look for excessive repetition
    • look for missing details, fill in gaps
    • redo problems you got wrong
    • what section/concept does each problem come from and how does the problem fit into a bigger picture?
  • understand course material as an integrated whole
    • both the big picture and execution of the details are important!!
    • refine and revise your idea of the big picture
    • work on the details by doing extra problems in areas of weakness
  • discuss!
    • in office hours
    • with friends
    • with yourself

V. Mastery (exam preparation)

  • repeat everything above
  • don't give up, persistence matters
  • don't cram, plan in advance
  • get good rest before the exam
  • Remember, studying is an extended activity spanning ALL SEMESTER starting before class (see above) NOT a few days or hours before the exam -- why?? -- YOUR BRAIN NEEDS TIME to absorb the material and to truly learn on a deep level.
